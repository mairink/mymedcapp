  1. Self Employment details ( Applicant )

As a self employed person you complete Form 11 annually and submit to Revenue for assessment. Revenue assess the Form 11 and may determine no return of income is required for a set period and issue a Non Liable Notification Letter. Alternatively revenue will issue a Notice of Assessment which is required to determine your net weekly income for this online medical card application.

You can enter the net weekly income amount or alternatively click on "Show Calculator" and use it to calculate the weekly income amount. Simply type in the amounts from your notice of assessment and click the "Calculate" button.

When you have finished typing in the net weekly income amount or have used the calculator please click on "Save and Return"
